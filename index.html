About Bhutanese Spicy Chips

Our story and passion for authentic Bhutanese flavors

Our Story

Bhutanese Spicy Chips was born from a passion for authentic Bhutanese flavors and a desire to share them with the world. Founded in Phuentsholing in 2023, our business started as a small home kitchen experiment and has grown into a beloved local snack brand.

Our Mission

We aim to preserve and promote Bhutanese culinary traditions by creating high-quality, flavorful snacks that showcase our unique spices. Every bag of Druk Spicy Chips is made with care, using traditional recipes and locally sourced ingredients.

Why Choose Us

  • Authentic Bhutanese spice blends
  • Locally sourced ingredients
  • Small-batch production for quality
  • Supporting local farmers
  • Passion for preserving Bhutanese flavors
